•Debtonator Privacy Policy•

  1. Introduction

    This privacy policy tells you about the way Debtonator (we, our or us), collects information, holds it, uses it, and if we disclose it, who we disclose it to.

  2. You own your data

    Debtonator is a Debtor reminder service which stores information about a company or individual (Organisation) as provided or entered by that company or individual. Debtonator will only store information that is entered by the Users of an Organisation, or automatically imported at a User’s instruction. The data entered, or imported on instruction, by the Users of an Organisation remains the property of the Organisation and Debtonator will not use nor make available for use any of this information without permission of the Organisation.

  3. You control who has access to your data

    The data entered, or imported on instruction, by the Users of an Organisation is stored securely in a database and is only accessible to the approved Users of the Organisation. A user name and password is required in order to access an Organisation's information in Debtonator.

    It is the responsibility of users to keep their passwords safe. It is the responsibility of the initial registrar of the Organisation on Debtonator to ensure that any Users that are invited to use the Organisation’s account have permission to view the Organisation’s information stored in the Debtonator account.

  4. Debtonator monitors system usage

    Debtonator has access to and may use aggregate information such as number of associated Users, number of transactions and billing information for the purpose of billing and monitoring server and software performance as well as for other internal purposes of Debtonator.

    Debtonator staff and key commercial partners can access non-identifying and aggregated usage information and transaction volumes in order to better understand how our customers are using Debtonator so we can improve the system design and where appropriate have the system prompt users with suggestions on ways to improve their own use of the system. All aggregated usage information is stored in a secure Debtonator data warehouse facility.

  5. Your data is sent securely across the internet

    Debtonator Servers have commercial security certificates so all data transferred between the Users of an organisation and the Debtonator Servers is encrypted. However, the internet is not in itself a secure environment. Users should only enter, or instruct the importation of, data to the database within a secure environment. This means that the User’s browser must support the encryption security used in connection with the Debtonator Servers.

    Debtonator stores your Data in the location Debtonator considers will give the Organisation and its Users most efficient access from wherever in the World they may be. Access and storage controls via the Software and the Website are however administered by Debtonator in England as the holder of that Data. Debtonator's address and contact details are available on our contact page. Where an Organisation or User accesses or inputs Data from somewhere other than the country where the Data is stored or to be stored, the Organisation and the User consent to that Data being transferred from one country to the other (including via any intermediate country) as a function of transmission across the internet.

  6. Debtonator does not store your credit card details

    If you pay for Debtonator by Credit Card, your credit card details are not stored by Debtonator and cannot be accessed by Debtonator staff.

  7. Debtonator will give you access to your data at any time

    On request, Debtonator will provide the Client with a full export of the Organisation’s data in a common file format determined by Debtonator.

    The Organisation’s data may be permanently deleted by Debtonator 30 days after the Organisation stops paying to use Debtonator, or at the Clients request (a fee may be applicable for such requests).

    Your browser stores only enough information to keep you logged in.

    Debtonator uses temporary cookies to keep a session open after a User logs in. These cookies do not contain any information about the User or the Organisation and will automatically expire at a predetermined time after the session is finished.

    Debtonator may collect and hold information relating to you that you have provided to us, that is in the public domain or from when you have used our website.

    We collect other information to help us understand how our site is working and how we could improve it and/or our services. While we may publish aggregated information, we will not publish individuals information. If you post messages to any of our social media locations, we collect and may keep the information in those messages.

    Debtonator may use this information for a variety of purposes related to contacting you but not limited to this.
